# Welcome to the DotCalendar wiki!

DotCalendar is a lightweight macOS menu bar app that generates a dynamic GitHub-style dot calendar wallpaper. Each dot represents a day of the year — past days are filled, today is highlighted, and future days are dimmed.

The wallpaper automatically switches between light and dark variants based on your system appearance.

## Requirements

- macOS 14.0 (Sonoma) or later
- Apple Silicon (arm64)

## Quick Start

1. Download `DotCalendar.dmg` from [Releases](https://github.com/wibawasuyadnya/DotCalendar/releases)
2. Drag **DotCalendar** to **Applications**
3. Launch — wallpaper is generated automatically
